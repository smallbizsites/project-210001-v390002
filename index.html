Stay Organized: Tax Calendar Subscription

Never miss a deadline. Automatically add important tax dates to your preferred calendar app.

Outlook (365, Desktop, Web, Mobile)

  1. Open Outlook.
  2. Go to Calendar.
  3. Choose “Add calendar” → “Subscribe from web”.
  4. Paste the calendar link and add it.

Apple Calendar (iPhone, iPad, Mac)

  1. Open the Calendar app.
  2. Mac: Choose File → New Calendar Subscription. iPhone/iPad: Tap “Add Subscription Calendar”.
  3. Paste the calendar link and subscribe.

Google Calendar

  1. Go to Google Calendar.
  2. On the left, click “+” next to “Other calendars” and choose From URL.
  3. Paste the calendar link and click Add calendar.
